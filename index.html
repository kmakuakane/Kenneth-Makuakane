About

Behind the songs & poems

I’m Your Artist Name, a songwriter and poet who lives in the space where melody and language become the same thing. I write songs that feel like diary entries, and poems that sound like they could be sung under low-lit skies.

This site is my home base: a place where you can stream the music, read the lyrics like little stories, and wander through the poems that never needed instruments—only breath, memory, and time.

If something here finds you at the right moment, let it stay. Share it with someone else who might need it. That’s how these quiet things travel.
